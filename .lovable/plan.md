## Design Theme
- **Dark background** (near-black) with **neon green/yellow accent color** (#CCFF00-style)
- Bold, uppercase typography with letter-spacing for headings
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects
- Minimalist, high-contrast UI with glowing/neon effects on interactive elements
